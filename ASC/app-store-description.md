## Description

**Preview, compare, and discover every font on your device.**

TypeFace is the essential typography tool for designers and creators. Browse all iOS system fonts, preview them with your own text, and compare side-by-side to find the perfect typeface for your project.

**KEY FEATURES:**

🔤 **Browse All Fonts**
Explore every system font installed on your device. Search by name, filter by style, and preview each one instantly.

✍️ **Live Text Preview**
Type your own text and see it rendered in any font in real-time. Perfect for testing headlines, body copy, and more.

⚖️ **Side-by-Side Compare**
Compare 2-4 fonts simultaneously with the same text. The fastest way to choose between similar typefaces.

📐 **Scene Templates**
See fonts in real-world contexts: titles, body text, logos, posters, social media posts, and business cards.

🎚 **Typography Controls**
Fine-tune every detail — font size, weight, letter spacing, line height, color, and alignment.

📤 **Export Comparisons**
Export your font comparisons as high-resolution PNG images to share with clients or your team.

⭐ **Favorites & Collections**
Save your favorite fonts and create custom collections of font pairs and trios.

📋 **Font Details**
View complete font metadata including family info, available style variants, and character set previews.

🌗 **Dark Mode**
Full dark mode support for comfortable use in any lighting.

**PERFECT FOR:**
• Graphic designers choosing typefaces
• UI/UX designers building design systems
• Social media managers creating content
• Students making presentations and posters
• App developers selecting system fonts
• Content creators crafting visual posts
